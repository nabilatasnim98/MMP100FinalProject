Ribbit & Co.

[Scene: A serene pond with lily pads. Ribbit, a quirky frog wearing glasses, sits on a lily pad with a book titled "Frogosophy." Nearby, Co., a mischievous frog, hides behind a bush.]

Ribbit: "Ah, the joy of reading by the pond."

[Co. sneaks up, wearing a fake mustache and a bowler hat, trying to impersonate Ribbit.]

Co.: "I am Ribbit! Watch me croak and read at the same time!"

[Ribbit looks bemused as Co. hops around doing a bizarre impression, causing nearby frogs to laugh.]

Ribbit: "Co., that's not... remotely accurate."

[Co. falls into the pond, glasses askew, and Ribbit laughs heartily.]

Ribbit: "Looks like someone just took a dive into comedy!"
